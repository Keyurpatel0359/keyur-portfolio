Alzero - Case Study

A concept food delivery app inspired by Zomato, designed for ordering meals with a modern, user-friendly experience.

Project Overview

Alzero is a food delivery app concept that allows users to browse restaurants, discover new cuisines, and place online orders easily. The focus was to create a seamless, engaging UI that makes food discovery simple, fast, and visually delightful.

Problem Statement

While many food apps exist, users often struggle with cluttered interfaces, slow navigation, and overwhelming options. There’s a need for a clean design that highlights food discovery, quick re-ordering, and smooth checkout.

Objective

The goal was to design a modern mobile app with a minimal yet colorful interface. Alzero focuses on ease of use, personalized food recommendations, and a fast ordering process.

Target Users

  • Busy professionals ordering quick meals
  • Students looking for affordable and fast food options
  • Families ordering group meals and combos
  • Food lovers discovering new restaurants

Design Process

  • Concept: A fictional case study for UI/UX practice.
  • Wireframes: Focused on home feed, restaurant details, and checkout experience.
  • UI Design: Bright, appetizing color palette with rounded cards and bold food imagery.

Key Features

  • Browse restaurants & cuisines with filters
  • Search food items quickly
  • Restaurant details with reviews, menu, and ratings
  • Cart & checkout flow
  • Live order tracking
  • Favorites and re-ordering option
