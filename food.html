Online Food Delivery App - Case Study

Creating a smooth and efficient food ordering experience.

Project Overview

Design a user-friendly online food delivery app aimed at streamlining the ordering process and improving customer satisfaction. The focus was on simplifying navigation, reducing friction during checkout, and providing clear delivery updates.

Problem Statement

Users often encounter complex menus, slow order processes, and lack of transparency during delivery, leading to dissatisfaction and abandoned carts.

Objective

Build an intuitive app that offers faster ordering with minimal steps, quick reordering for frequent customers, and real-time delivery tracking.

Target Users

  • Busy professionals needing quick meal deliveries during work hours.
  • Students ordering snacks and late-night meals frequently.

Process

  • Research: Conducted interviews with 8 users; 75% want faster reordering options; 60% dislike promotional clutter.
  • User Personas: Mark (24), student; Emily (29), project manager ordering during lunch breaks.
  • User Flow: Home > Browse Restaurants > Menu > Cart > Payment > Delivery Tracking.
  • Wireframes: Simple home screen, favorites shortcut, and delivery tracking map.
  • Visual Design: Used warm, inviting colors (orange & white) with clean typography and large product imagery.
  • Prototype: Interactive Figma prototype showcasing smooth browsing and checkout flow.

Challenges

Balancing the need for a fast checkout process while collecting necessary delivery and payment information.

Key Learnings

Quick-access buttons like "Reorder" improve customer retention. Reducing visual clutter helps guide users through key actions like adding items to the cart and completing checkout.
