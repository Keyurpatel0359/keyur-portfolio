RayBright - Case Study

A fictional mobile app project designed for a solar panel cleaning service.

Project Overview

RayBright is an imaginary mobile app concept designed for a solar panel cleaning service. The purpose of this project was to explore how a service-based business might operate through a mobile-first experience. It focuses on booking services, viewing pricing packages, and connecting with the company—all through an easy-to-use mobile interface.

Problem Statement

Customers often find it difficult to schedule and manage solar panel cleaning services through traditional methods. There's also a lack of clarity around service pricing and package details.

Objective

The goal was to design a smooth, modern mobile app UI for a fictional solar panel cleaning service. The app allows users to log in, view pricing, select service packages, and book services—all from a mobile device.

Target Users

  • Homeowners with solar panels.
  • Industrial clients needing maintenance.
  • Anyone looking for reliable solar panel cleaning services.

Design Process

  • Concept Only: This is not a real-world product, but a design concept to showcase mobile app UI/UX skills.
  • Wireframes: Basic flows for login, service selection, and booking.
  • UI Design: Bright, gradient-themed mobile screens with consistent visual language.
  • Prototype: Designed in Figma with all screens linked to simulate the user journey.

Key Features

  • Login & Sign-Up screens
  • Pricing and service package display
  • Booking form with package selection
  • Company information and contact details
