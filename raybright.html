RayBright Website Redesign - Case Study

Revamping a corporate website for better branding, responsiveness, and UX clarity.

Project Overview

The goal was to redesign the RayBright website to reflect a more modern, professional look that aligns with the company's core services in solar and sustainable energy solutions.

Problem Statement

The old design lacked brand clarity, consistency, and responsiveness across devices. Users found navigation confusing and key service information buried in text-heavy layouts.

Objective

Create a clean, responsive, and visually consistent corporate site that communicates RayBright’s value clearly, improves trust, and supports business inquiries.

Target Users

  • Business partners looking for reliable energy solutions.
  • Eco-conscious individuals researching solar installation services.

Design Process

  • Research: Competitive audit, old website review, user interviews.
  • Wireframes: Homepage, About, and Services pages focusing on visual hierarchy and clarity.
  • Visual Design: Light-themed interface with green/blue accents and professional typography.
  • Prototype: Interactive mockup designed in Figma demonstrating smooth navigation.

Challenges

Balancing corporate tone with user-friendly visual elements and ensuring fast performance on mobile devices.

Key Learnings

Minimal design, aligned branding, and clear CTAs significantly improved trust and engagement. Clients responded positively to the new layout and responsiveness.
